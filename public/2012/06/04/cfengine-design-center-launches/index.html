CFEngine Design Center Launches

Jun 4, 2012

If you follow CFEngine you may have notices that the multiple pay versions have been condensed down to CFEngine Enterprise with the latest release. You may not have noticed that the CFEngine Design Center also launched.

Don’t get me wrong, I am excited about the new 25 free enterprise nodes and the new features in the Enterprise edition, but I see greater value in the Design Center. This is a great resource for new and old users. Wrap up common tasks in modularized bundles and shove the details out of sight. Not only is it a place to find reusable policy to accomplish a specific task but its also a wealth of policy to look at when designing your own infrastructure. Benefit from others contributions and support the community by contributing policy or even bug reports.
